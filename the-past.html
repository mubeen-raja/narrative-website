The Past

The Discovery of Our Solar System

  • Mercury

    1691

    In 1691, Thomas Harriott and Galileo Galilei first observed Mercury with the newly invented telescope.NASA, 2022

  • Venus

    650 BC

    In 650 BC, Mayan astronomers, who are Ancient observers, make their first detailed observations of Venus. This lead to the formation of a highly accurate calendar. NASA, 2022

  • Earth

    The Big Bang Theory

    The Big Bang is a theory in astronomy that states the universe originated billions of years ago in a rapid expansion from a single point of energy density. Dan Vergano, Cincinnati.com, 14 Mar. 2018

  • Mars

    Ancient Egypt

    The first recorded observations of Mars date as far back as the era of ancient Egypt over 4,000 years ago. NASA, 2022

  • Jupiter

    1610

    In 1610, Galileo Galilei makes the first detailed observations of Jupiter. NASA, 2022

  • Saturn

    700 BCE

    Around ~700 BCE, the oldest written records that document Saturn are attributed to the Assyrians. They had described the ringed planet as a sparkle in the night and named it "Star of Ninib." NASA, 2022

  • Uranus

    1781

    On March 13, 1781, British astronomer William Herschel discovers Uranus. It is the first new planet discovered since ancient times. NASA, 2022

  • Neptune

    1846

    In 1846, by using mathematical calculations, astronomers discover Neptune. This increased the number of known planets to eight. NASA, 2022
